PARC: Proximity

Google

google.com

The google website is a perfect example of "Proximity" because everything is given its own area on the page. Each section is specific to its own purpose. The webpage is a simple design. It has easy access to all of the features without any confusion.

White Space and Clean Design

Amazon

amazon.com

The Amazon website has perfect spacing and white space to keep everything well organized. It is very easy to see each product when you search for them. They are seperated by boxes and white space to show a clear selection.

PARC: Contrast

Star Wars Offical

starwars.com

The Star Wars website has a nice white text over a black background. It contrasts very well and this helps the viewer read it better. Especally on phones, I feel a black background makes it easier to read. Another featuere of contrast is the high quality pictures that pop on the screen when the background is dark.
